Our Privacy Commitment

At Aelius AI, we are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. Our approach to privacy is built on the following principles:

1. Transparency: We are clear about what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Minimalism: We only collect the data that is absolutely necessary for providing our services.

3. Security: We employ industry-leading security measures to protect your data.

4. User Control: We give you control over your data and respect your privacy choices.

Data Collection

Aelius AI is committed to minimal data collection. We only collect:

1. Account Information: Basic details needed to create and manage your account.

2. User Preferences: Settings you choose to customize your experience.

3. Usage Data: We may monitor your usage patterns to determine compliance with our policies.

We do not collect or store any user-generated content, conversations, or queries for training purposes or any other reason beyond the immediate processing required to provide our service.

Data Usage

The limited data we collect is used exclusively for:

1. Providing and improving our AI services

2. Personalizing your experience based on your preferences

3. Account management and security

4. Communicating important updates about our services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for any purpose.

Data Security

Protecting your data is our highest priority. We employ robust security measures, including:

1. End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions

2. Regular security audits and penetration testing

3. Strict access controls and authentication procedures

4. Continuous monitoring for potential security threats

5. Regular data backup and disaster recovery planning

Policy Updates

We are committed to maintaining transparency about our privacy practices:

1. Notification: Users will be notified of any significant changes to this policy.

2. Review Period: We provide a 30-day review period for major policy updates.

3. Feedback: We welcome user feedback on our privacy practices and policy updates.

4. Version History: A record of policy changes is maintained and accessible to users.

5. Clarification: Our support team is available to address any questions about our privacy policy.
